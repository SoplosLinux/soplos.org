Technical Support & Bugs

Please do not use email for technical support or bug reports.

To ensure you get the best help and that fixes benefit everyone, please use our public channels:

  • Community Forums: soplos.org/forums (General help, questions)
  • GitHub Issues: github.com/SoplosLinux (Bug reports, feature requests)
Reporting a Bug?  Please include your system info, steps to reproduce, and screenshots if possible.
