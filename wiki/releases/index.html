Soplos Plymouth Manager 2.0.0

Comprehensive rewrite of the Plymouth theme visualization and management engine with advanced Wayland support.

  • Modular Architecture: New decoupled core that improves stability and facilitates the implementation of future features.
  • Wayland Support: Native and stable screenshot capture in GNOME (Boro) environments under the Wayland protocol.
  • Intelligent Previews: Optimized rendering engine with automatic environment detection (Xfce, KDE, GNOME).
  • TTY Management: Improved preview for console themes and correction of the duplicated password prompt.
